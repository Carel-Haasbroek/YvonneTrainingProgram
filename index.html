Yvonne training program

Some extra stuff

  1. Remember, to achieve weight loss while training hard, you either need to train more or eat less. Not both.
  2. Don't rest less than 2 min. Rest is just as important as the volume of training.
  3. Sets/reps format is Sets x Reps. Meaning that 3x4 means 3 sets of 4 reps.

  • Morning Exercises:

    These exercises should not take a lot of energy or time. The goal of these is to help condition the body for more intense training down the line. The athlete should still have energy for the day and afternoon training after this.

    3x 3 min jump rope
    20x bridge
    20x shrimp
    (best time to drill basics, include other drills you do with your jitz class)
    2x 2 min plank. if you cant make the whole time, rest 10-20 secs then try again as the timer tics.
    2x 2 min bicycle crunch
    2x 2 min russian twists. one each side

    Bench mark exercises:
    Pushups till collapse
    Situps till collapse
    The goal of these are to track your improvement as you go on in the year. The ultimate goal is a really big number where its no longer an exercise but a formality. You will begin strong, then dip for a few weeks (2-4), and only then should you see consistent improvement.

  • Strength Day:

    10 min treadmill/bicycle

      Do one every week and the other every other week
    • 5x5 dead lifts @ 20-40% bodyweight (one of the best exercises to build static strength)
    • 5x5 cleans @ 10-20% bodyweight (one of the best exercises to build static strength)
      • Also note, these are LOW weights Build up strength and technique for the first 6 month, and only then increase in weight.
    5 min rows
    3x8 Pull downs (on any weight you feel comfortable with. eventually these must become pull ups)
    NOTE: ALL these exercises pulls on your back, so pay CLOSE attention to your posture while doing these
